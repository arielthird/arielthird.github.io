My favourite movie: Disney's Hercules (1997)

It's Disney's retelling of the Greek demigod, Hercules. It has fantastic musical numbers and Danny DeVito. There's essentially no better movie.

Main Cast List:

  • James Woods as Hades
  • Danny DeVito as Phil
  • Susan Egan as Megara
  • Tate Donovan as Hercules
  • Roger Bart as Hercules (singing voice)
  • Rip Torn as Zeus
